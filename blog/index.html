Rainmeter: Customizing my desktop

Rainmeter is an open source desktop customization tool with a large community built around it creating and sharing custom "skins" for anyone to use, usually shared on DeviantArt. Skins are really just sets of various .ini files defining what to display and how to display it, in an easy to understand format. Editing the files is very simple, though, basic coding skills reccomended. Overall it's just an amazing program and another example of the power of open source.
